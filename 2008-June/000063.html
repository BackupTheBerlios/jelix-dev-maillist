The new repository is ok

For those who gave me their ssh key, the address is :

svn+ssh://svnjelix@svn.jelix.org/jelix/

for others

http://svn.jelix.org/jelix/


You can "relocate" your local copy to avoid to do a full checkout. Example:

svn switch --relocate https://svn.berlios.de/svnroot/repos/jelix/trunk 
svn+ssh://svnjelix@svn.jelix.org/jelix/trunk .

Note that the user is svnjelix, not your berlios login. The server will 
recognize your real login with your ssh key (so in the svn log, your old 
berlios name will be used instead of "svnjelix")


We leave berlios.de because there are too many problem with there 
subversion server. But we keep files and the mailing list on berlios.de.
